About Coaching

AYSO coaching is built for everyday people - NO EXPERIENCE NEEDED - because our Region gives you all the training, tools, and support to grow into an amazing coach. You won’t be alone at any point; from age-appropriate AYSO courses to on-field guidance and a community of volunteers, we walk with you every step of the journey. Coaching becomes a chance to bond with your child, create joyful memories, and help kids develop confidence, teamwork, and a love of the game. AYSO’s nationally recognized training is designed specifically for volunteers with little or no soccer background, so if you bring enthusiasm and a willingness to learn, we can turn you into a certified, capable coach who truly brings out the best in kids.

Coach Conduct

Principles

  • POSITIVE COACHING - Bring encouragement, patience, and praise to every player.
  • BE PREPARED & ORGANIZED - Have a plan for each practice and keep rosters handy.
  • POSITIVE SIDELINE CONDUCT - Keep comments supportive and model good behavior.
  • RESPECT THE REFEREES - Treat referees with kindness and support their calls.
  • ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY - No yelling at refs or negative comments toward players, coaches, or parents.
  • GOOD COMMUNICATION - Keep parents and coordinators informed about schedules and changes.
  • EXPECTATIONS - Set simple expectations: effort, respect, teamwork, and positivity.
  • ALWAYS HAVE PLAYER FORMS - Bring registration and emergency forms to every session.
  • GET YOUR PARENTS INVOLVED - Ask for help with setup, snacks, and cleanup.
  • REMEMBER: THE KIDS ARE HERE TO HAVE FUN! - Focus on joy, confidence, and friendships over winning.
