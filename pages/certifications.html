Certifications

Required courses, background checks, and steps to get certified as a coach or referee.

Coach Certification Requirements

AYSO requires all coaches — Head and Assistant — to be fully certified before working with players. All training is free and accessed through your Volunteer account using the AYSOU button.

Below are the 9 required steps to become a certified coach for the 2026 season.

Accessing AYSOU (Training Platform)

Before completing any training, make sure you can access AYSOU.

How to Access AYSOU (Sports Connect Regions like AYSO 779)

  • Log in to your Sports Connect account at ayso779.org
  • Go to your Volunteer page and click the AYSOU button
  • Accept the Terms & Conditions on your first visit
  • Select Training Library to begin your courses
  • If the AYSOU button is missing, follow the troubleshooting steps on the AYSO Wiki or contact support

Important Notes

  • Volunteers must log in to AYSOU at least once every 18 months to avoid being archived
  • If your account becomes archived, log back in — your certifications will reappear within 1–2 days

Certification Requirements

  • Complete the 2026 Volunteer Form (signing up)
    • Log in or create an account at ayso779.org
    • Required once you’ve been assigned to a team
    • Must be completed every season
  • Safe Haven & Related Trainings
    • Safe Haven Certification — If completed in 2016 or later, you do not need to retake it. Go to AYSOU → Training Library → Safe Haven Courses and complete all sections.
    • Concussion Awareness Training (One Time Only) — Go to AYSOU → Training Library → Safe Haven → CDC Concussion Awareness Training (required once; does not expire).
    • Sudden Cardiac Arrest Training — Go to AYSOU → Training Library → Safe Haven Courses → Sudden Cardiac Arrest and complete all sections.
    • SafeSport Training — Go to AYSOU → Training Library → Safe Haven → Access Your SafeSport Course Here (required annually for volunteers age 18+).
  • Background Check (Risk Status)
    • In your Volunteer Account, go to Risk Status
    • Select Renew & Update
    • Complete all linked steps
  • Live Scan Fingerprinting
    • Required by California law
    • Contact your Division Coordinator for instructions and available locations
  • Upload a Profile Photo
    • In your Sports Connect account, upload a headshot
    • This photo appears on your coach badge
  • Division‑Specific Coach Training
    • Go to Training Library → Coaches
    • Select the course for the division you are coaching (6U, 8U, 10U, etc.)
    • Must be completed before the first game (8U is minimum for all 8U and up coaches)
