Practice Guidelines – Spring Season

Rules, field notes, and restricted locations for spring practice scheduling.

General Rules

  • Coaches select their own practice days, times, and locations.
  • Teams may hold up to 2 practices per week.
  • 8U and younger are limited to 2 total hours of practice per week.
Ayala Park – Chino (Wednesdays Only)

We are guests at Ayala Park, and space is limited. Please be courteous to all teams using the area and work together to make the limited field options work for everyone. A little flexibility and communication go a long way in helping every team get the practice time they need.

Fields: 8 & 9

Available Times:

  • 5:00–6:00 PM
  • 6:00–7:00 PM
  • 7:00–8:00 PM

Division Notes

  • 10U–14U: Contact your division coordinator for field assignment.
  • 8U and below: May use the grass areas surrounding Fields 8 & 9.

Helpful Tip

Ayala Park can be a great option for your team until Daylight Saving Time begins. After that, you’ll have more flexibility to choose an alternate location, day, and time that works best for your group.

Not Practicing at Ayala Park

If you are not utilizing Ayala Park, practicing within Chino Hills is essential. Please ensure:

  • Your practice location has restroom facilities.
  • You are mindful that lighting is limited until March.
  • You are not practicing at any of the restricted locations listed below.

Restricted Locations (Spring Season)

The following fields and parks may NOT be used for practices during the spring:
