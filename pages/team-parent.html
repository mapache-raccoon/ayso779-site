Team Parent

Team Parents keep a team organized and supported so coaches can focus on the players.

Volunteer Today

What a Team Parent Does

  • Coordinate snacks and water schedules.
  • Manage team communications (email/text group).
  • Help organize volunteer coverage for games.
  • Serve as a point of contact with coaches and league staff.

Time Commitment

Typically a small weekly time commitment during the season; most tasks are flexible.
